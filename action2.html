Take action
Share your values.
Knowing where you stand is important to us. We want to support brands that believe in what we believe in. When you create a space for values in your company messaging, it shows us that you care about more than just sales or earnings. It shows us that you care about how you conduct business, about doing things the right way.
Gen Z has a high BS meter.
We fact check what brands say with what they do, who they partner with, and what their profits support. So when you speak, do it intentionally, with authenticity and purpose, because being genuine is how you earn our respect and our business.
We respect transparency.
More than anything else, we appreciate when brands are honest with us. Whether that’s owning your mistakes, defending your beliefs, or publicizing company policies, we’ll stand by brands that stand by their values. So, if you place honesty above appearances, if nothing else, you’ll earn our respect—transparency is a small price to pay for that.
