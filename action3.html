Take action
Understand there’s no back to normal.
Gen Z’s standards have changed. And with expectations for brands higher than ever, superficial solutions will only hurt you down the road. Don’t think that the crisis marketing tactics of yesterday will continue to work today. Make sure your adjustments go beyond hashtags and emotive commercials; your fundamental strategies should be examined, and more than likely changed.
We buy brands that buy into our ideals.
The world won’t save itself and we don’t intend to let it—we’re committed to creating a tomorrow that’s better than today. And we bring this mindset to which brands we support. Brands that make a conscious effort to leave this planet better than they found it through action quickly gain Gen Z’s approval.
Emphasize thoughtfulness over immediacy.
When a crisis strikes, the instinct is often to respond quickly. But, the key to a well-received response is its thoughtfulness, rather than its speed. Meaningful action taken over time earns more goodwill with Gen Z than quick solutions that solve little.
