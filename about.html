Who we are
We are Jack Brosnan, Gilly Cole, Joseph Han, Tracee Hartley, Cara Jones, Ryan King, Amber Little, Raven Mo and Morgan O’Connor.
Working in advertising, we’ve seen the good that brands can do when they speak up and take action. And we’ve felt the harm when they don’t. We created the idea of Silence Speaks because we believe in brands and their ability to make the world a better place.

As Gen Z creatives, we understand the goals of brands and we live with the challenges facing our generation. We want to bridge that gap and create a relationship where both parties, and the world, thrive.
