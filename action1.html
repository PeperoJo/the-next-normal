    Action 1
    Unlearn to relearn
    “The most important thing a brand can do right now, especially with young consumers, is listen.”
    — Chief Operating Officer, Gen Z Marketing Agency
    Take action
    The people in the communities know best.
    something
    If you’re just discovering how deeply ingrained social injustice is in our country, it can be overwhelming. You may feel like you need to be an expert, but entire communities have been living this reality their whole lives. As a brand you have the power and opportunity to amplify the voices of these individuals, rather than drowning them out.
    Gen Z is the most vocal generation in history.
    something
    We’re willing to share our thoughts on social media, in focus groups, and anywhere people or brands will listen. This presents a unique opportunity for your brand to get authentic, actionable insights. And if you pay attention, you can make decisions and implement changes that meaningfully engage us. Our words can positively impact your responses to social injustice and business problems alike—get Gen Z in the room, and listen.
    We’re looking for allies, not friends.
    something
    Being an ally doesn’t mean that you’ve experienced the same situations as people in marginalized communities—it means knowing that you haven’t. Gen Z is looking for brands to understand that they don’t understand, and find ways to support communities instead of co-opting the conversation.
